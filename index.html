Cheddar Ranch Popcorn Chicken Recipe

Serving 4

I found this recipe from youtube channel called "Tasty"

Ingridients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 packet ranch dressing mix
  • 2 cups cheese crackers
  • Oil for frying

Preparation

  1. Chop chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces.
  2. in a large bowl, combine chicken, buttermilk, and ranch packet, and refregiate for 2 hours.
  3. In a large ziplock bag, crumble the cheese crackers into desired crumbs (to replace bread crumbs).
  4. Place cracker crumbs into a medium bowl and coat each piece of chicken until fully covered.
  5. Heat oil to 175Â°C and fry chicken until crackers turn golden brown (about 3 minutes).
  6. Place on a paper towel to dry.
  7. Enjoy with a side of ranch or any sauce!
